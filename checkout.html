Colombian Pottery

Terracota Pots straight from the Colombian Jungles!

I built a website for an Artisan based in Medellin, Colombia. I am selling his products because I believe in his talent and he askedme to build a website for him, where he can sell the first one of a kind products of this collection. The first thing is learning the products and what they are made of and represent. All Colombian Boho pottery is handmade with natural clay by Colombian artisans, who have been making traditional terracotta crafts forcenturies. By buying our pots, you support a local community of craftsmen in Colombia and help secure the further existence of their beautiful craft.
